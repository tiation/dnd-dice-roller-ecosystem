Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

D&D Dice Roller is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Data: Dice roll history is stored locally on your device
  • App Usage: Basic analytics to improve app performance (anonymous)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to fix bugs and improve stability

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the dice rolling functionality
  • Store your roll history locally on your device
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Provide customer support when requested

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Roll history is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers
  • We do not collect personal information like names, emails, or contact details
  • Anonymous usage analytics help us improve the app experience

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for:

  • Google Play Services (for app distribution and updates)
  • Analytics services (anonymous usage data only)

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • Clear roll history within the app settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@tiation.com
