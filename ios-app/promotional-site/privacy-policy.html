Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

D&D Dice Roller for iOS is designed with privacy-first principles. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Data: Dice roll history is stored locally on your device using iOS Core Data
  • App Usage: Basic analytics to improve app performance (anonymous, via App Store Connect)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to fix bugs and improve stability (if you opt-in)

  • App Tracking Transparency: We do not track users across apps or websites
  • Local Storage: All data stays on your device using iOS secure storage
  • No Account Required: Use the app completely offline without sign-up

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the dice rolling functionality
  • Store your roll history locally on your device using iOS Core Data
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Provide customer support when requested through App Store

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Roll history is encrypted and stored locally using iOS Keychain and Core Data
  • We do not collect personal information like names, emails, or contact details
  • Anonymous usage analytics help us improve the app experience (via Apple's privacy-preserving analytics)
  • All data is protected by iOS security features including Face ID/Touch ID if enabled

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use these Apple and third-party services:

  • App Store Connect (for app distribution and anonymous analytics)
  • iOS System Services (for haptic feedback and sound)
  • No third-party advertising networks or tracking services

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, complying with COPPA and Apple's guidelines.

6. Your Rights Under iOS

You have the right to:

  • Delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • Clear roll history within the app settings
  • Control app permissions through iOS Settings
  • Opt-out of crash reporting through iOS Settings
  • Request data deletion (though data is stored locally)

7. Apple App Store Privacy

This app complies with Apple's App Store privacy requirements:

  • Privacy labels accurately reflect our data collection
  • No data is linked to user identity
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • Local storage only, no server transmission

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date and through App Store updates.
